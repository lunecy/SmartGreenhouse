About Us

The Greenhouse is currently an essential part of the agricultural process across the globe. Such greenhouses require constant monitoring to maintain a healthy plant growth. For this purpose, the team is designing a miniature automated greenhouse which can be used for monitoring purposes. The project requires building a system which can monitor the ambient temperature, the soil moisture level and the concentration of carbon dioxide in the air. This report will present the progress regarding the design and implementation in Phase 2. The report also discusses the future plans for Phase 3 along with all the deviations that took place. Finally the CEAB graduate attributes are completed to the environmental impact of this system.
